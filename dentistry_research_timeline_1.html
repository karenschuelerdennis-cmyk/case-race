Reading the figure. The time axis is schematic: the five eras are drawn at equal width and are not to linear scale, so that recent veterinary milestones remain legible alongside millennia of human dental history. A lane's line is dashed and grey through periods with no dedicated research literature for that species, and turns solid and colored once research begins — making visible that the eras apply very differently across species. Era boundaries are a synthesis drawn from where the field-defining papers and classification shifts fall, not a published canonical periodization. Flagged citations (e.g. Miller 1901, Andreasen 1970) should be verified against the primary source.
